## How It Works section

  

**Section label:** How I Work

  

**Heading:** How we get there.

  

**Intro note:** To ensure your solution is built to last, I generally follow a straightforward process designed to identify and fix the root cause of your specific challenges.

  

**Step 01**

Title: Understand the situation

Body: A close look at what's actually there: your data, your processes, your team's specific needs, before any solution gets proposed. The work follows from that, not from a pre-built approach.

  

**Step 02**

Title: Build what fits

Body: The solution is designed around your specific situation. Not more complex than it needs to be, and not something that requires constant technical oversight to keep running.

  

**Step 03**

Title: Hand it over

Body: Documentation, training, and a system you can run in house. No requirement to have me on retainer to keep things operational. If you want help down the road, that option is there.
